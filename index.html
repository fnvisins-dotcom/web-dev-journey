From Divine Texts to Digital Code: My Unexpected Path

My Background

Years in Sacred Study

For five semesters, I immersed myself in Islamic theology. My days were filled with ancient texts, philosophical debates, and understanding the deeper meanings of faith. I learned to analyze complex arguments, respect diverse perspectives, and communicate profound ideas clearly.

But something was missing. I loved the learning, yet I felt a pull toward creating something tangible—something that could reach millions instantly.

The Turning Point

The AI Revolution Changed Everything

When ChatGPT launched, I saw the future. AI wasn't replacing humans—it was amplifying those who understood how to wield it. I realized theology gave me critical thinking, but tech would give me impact.

I made a decision: 6 months to become a full-stack developer. No looking back.
